The Hi Interviews Ecosystem

A strategic overview of our user journey, value delivery, and long-term retention model.

PHASE 1

Acquisition & Conversion

Discovery

"Monica" discovers our platform via targeted marketing, landing on index.html and exploring why.html.

Onboarding

She proceeds to login.html to create her account, a seamless and simple process.

Strategic Conversion

On payment.html, she selects a package. We highlight our flexible coupon system—a key tool that allows us to offer targeted discounts to maximize conversions.

PHASE 2

Core Service & Success

Activation

Her payment is confirmed, and she's redirected to her new profile.html dashboard.

Mentorship Loop

Monica engages in 1-to-1 training and uses Buddy Coins for interviews, building skills and confidence.

SUCCESS: Job Cracked!

Monica lands her dream job. This is our primary success metric and the foundation for our next phase.

PHASE 3

Retention & LTV Growth

The New Challenge

Months into her new role, Monica faces a complex task she has never seen before.

On-Demand Guide

She returns to her profile and uses our innovative "Book a Guide" service for real-time help on her specific work task.

Task Mastery & Trust

An expert helps her solve the ticket and teaches her the concepts, establishing us as a long-term career partner.

Support & Admin Backbone

User Support

Users access support via customer_chat.html, with clear expectations on response times.

Admin Panel

Our team uses admin_chat.html to manage all conversations efficiently, ensuring a high-quality support experience.
