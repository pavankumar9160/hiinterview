Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions, answered. Here’s some more information about our process and policies.

Do you guarantee a job?

No. Our goal is to raise your skill and confidence so that you perform your best. Job offers depend on company requirements, market conditions, and your performance on the day.

What do you do during interviews?

We guide you where it matters most—before the first round, between stages, and after it’s done. Our mentors help you stay calm, focused, and confident, so you walk into every interview prepared and in control.

How long does prep take?

Our standard training package is for 30 days. Most learners feel confident and ready within this timeframe, but the "Ultimate Advantage" package is fully flexible to your timeline.

Who are the mentors?

Our mentors are experienced engineers and professionals currently working in the industry. Each has over five years of experience and has conducted real interviews for their companies.
